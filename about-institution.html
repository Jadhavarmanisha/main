About

The Dnyandev Patil Charitable Trust, Chikhurda, Dist, Latur was established in September 1995. The trust is dedicated to educational, social and economic development of farming community and rural people. Trust started a college of Pharmacy Management comprising U.G. program i.e. D. Pharmacy (Diploma in Pharmacy) in September 2023 Institute No. 52362 and affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai to meet the human resources in the field of Pharmacy. Our motto to shape community, society through education, extension demonstration, training and make a perfect excellence in pharmacy sector and I assured our diploma holder are not only job seekers but also job creators.
