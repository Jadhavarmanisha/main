Welcome to KDP College of Pharmacy!

We aim to increase knowledge about drugs and medicines through high-quality teaching and research, thereby improving the quality of life through the best use of medicines.

The Dnyandev Patil Charitable Trust, Chikhurda, Dist, Latur was established in September 1995. The trust is dedicated to the educational, social, and economic development of the farming community and rural people. The trust started a College of Pharmacy Management offering the U.G. program, i.e., D. Pharmacy (Diploma in Pharmacy) in September 2023 (Institute No. 52362) and is affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai, to address the human resource needs in the field of Pharmacy.

Our History

Explore the journey of KDP College of Pharmacy and its milestones over the years.

2023

KDP College of Pharmacy was established in September 2023, offering the Diploma in Pharmacy (D. Pharmacy) program. The institute is affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE), Mumbai.

2018

Further expansion of the Trust’s outreach and services, strengthening partnerships with various educational and professional organizations.

2015

Continuous improvement and expansion of educational programs, with a focus on quality and relevance to community needs.

2010

Introduction of new programs and initiatives aimed at broadening the scope of education and training provided by the Trust.

2005

Development and enhancement of infrastructure and resources in the trust’s existing educational institutions to provide better facilities.

2000

The Trust expanded its educational initiatives, laying the groundwork for future institutions and programs to benefit the community.
